SM MEGAMALL

Your Ultimate Shopping & Leisure Destination

About

Opened in 1991, SM Megamall is a premier shopping destination located in the heart of Ortigas Center. As one of the largest malls in the world, it serves as a central hub for fashion, dining, and world-class entertainment.

Main Attractions

  • Mega Fashion Hall
  • IMAX Theater & Cinemas
  • Olympic-size Ice Skating Rink
  • SM Bowling Center

Why Visit?

Its strategic location makes it easily accessible from all parts of Metro Manila. Whether you are looking for global flagship stores, local delicacies, or a place to relax, SM Megamall provides a vibrant and modern experience that represents the growth of Pasig City.
