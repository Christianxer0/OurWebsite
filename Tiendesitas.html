TIENDESITAS

A Celebration of Filipino Culture & Craftsmanship

About

Tiendesitas is a unique shopping complex in Pasig City designed like a "village" featuring stylized Filipino architecture. With its Maranao-inspired pavilions and Bahay Kubo roots, it serves as a vibrant hub for local eats, live music, and native products.

Main Attractions

  • Handicrafts & Fashion Village
  • The Pet Village
  • Live Music Performances
  • Antiques & Garden Village

Why Visit?

Tiendesitas offers a mall experience like no other, blending traditional aesthetics with modern convenience. From its chemically-treated cogon grass roofs to the "Duyans" (hammocks) scattered around for shoppers, it is the perfect place to experience the warmth of Filipino hospitality and find locally-made treasures.
