RAINFOREST PARK

Experience Nature in the Heart of Pasig

About

Pasig Rainforest Park (RAVE) is an 8-hectare green oasis located in Maybunga. It serves as the city's primary recreational and natural park, offering a much-needed escape from the urban bustle while staying close to city amenities.

Main Attractions

  • Boating Lagoon & Zip Line
  • Mini Zoo & Botanical Garden
  • Adventure Park & Skatepark
  • Olympic-sized Swimming Pool

Why Visit?

Relaunched as the Rainforest Adventure Experience (RAVE) in 2013, the park has evolved into a complete family destination. Whether you are looking for a quiet stroll through the botanical gardens or an adrenaline-filled afternoon on the obstacle course, the park combines fitness, nature, and fun in one historic location.
