Relax & Explore

PASIG HANGOUTS

Discover the best parks, lifestyle centers, and places to chill in the city.

Nature Park

Rainforest Adventure Park

A massive nature park featuring a zoo, a playground, water park, and various eco-friendly attractions perfect for family weekends.

Modern Estate

Bridgetowne

A sprawling modern area bridging Pasig and Quezon City. Famous for its scenic parks, art installations, and stunning skyline views.

City Park

Ortigas Park

A relaxing, pocket open space right in the bustling heart of the Ortigas Central Business District. A perfect oasis for taking a quick break.

Lifestyle Area

Capitol Commons

A vibrant, upscale lifestyle destination featuring high-end shopping, diverse dining options, and wide open spaces for evening strolls.
