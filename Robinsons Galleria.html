ROBINSONS GALLERIA

A Landmark of Shopping & Heritage

About

Opened in 1990, Robinsons Galleria was the first flagship mall of Robinsons Malls. Located at the historic junction of EDSA and Ortigas Avenue, it has remained a cornerstone of the Ortigas Central Business District for decades.

Main Attractions

  • EDSA Shrine (Adjacent)
  • Movieworld Cinemas
  • PlayLab (Digital Playground)
  • Eat Street Food Hall

Why Visit?

Robinsons Galleria is more than just a mall; it is a lifestyle destination where modern commerce meets Philippine history. Being situated right beside the EDSA Shrine, it offers visitors a unique blend of high-end retail therapy and a chance to visit a significant religious and historical landmark.
